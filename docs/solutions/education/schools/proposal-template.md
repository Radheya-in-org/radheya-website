## What we will build

A custom website organized around five themes — each answering a question every parent silently asks.

### 1. The People — *"Who will teach my child?"*
- Faculty page with name, qualification, subject taught, and short bio
- Principal's and founder's message
- Alumni section — where they are now, what they are doing

### 2. The Place — *"Where will my child spend the day?"*
- Real classroom and infrastructure photos
- Library, science labs, computer labs
- Sports ground, indoor stadium, dining hall
- Cultural rooms, art studios, music spaces
- Optional: video tour of the campus

### 3. The Learning — *"How will my child be taught?"*
- A typical day at {SCHOOL_NAME}
- Your teaching methodology and academic approach
- Vision, mission, and founding principles
- School history — year by year

### 4. The Proof — *"Why this school?"*
- Toppers and academic results
- Sports achievements
- Events and cultural programs
- Public recognitions and awards
- News and announcements (your office staff updates these)

### 5. The Action — *"How do I enroll?"*
- Online admission inquiry form — routes instantly to your WhatsApp + email
- Multi-campus support, maps, directions
- Notices, holiday calendar, exam schedule
- Direct contact: principal, admissions, transport
